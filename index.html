What is this Website?

This is a "blog." Such relics of the past no longer exist in the modern world, having been driven obsolete by distasteful, postmodernist (and self-funded) thinkpieces, 280-character text limits and GPT-3 generated media. To embrace our colonial robot overlords, the name of this website was also generated by the Open-AI playground when fed the input

generate blog names based on the keywords cryptography, star trek, chess

In other words, this website is the truest form of artistic and cultural expression known to man: markdown and raw HTML, unread by all.

What do you write about?

A lot of stuff, actually. A comprehensive list of topics would be difficult to come up with, because it's largely whimsical musings on a topic-of-the-month along with the ocassional short story. I like to think of this as a personal diary. Because that's what it is, fundamentally -- some of these posts are likely to be ridiculously tiny, perhaps one-liners or Fellini quotes indicating what I felt like on, say, the 22nd of May 2023 (god forbid I have one of those time-travelling emails on the way to that date, because I probably do). That said, I suppose the general flavor of this website can probably be distilled down to at least half, if not all of the following subjects:

  • cryptography

  • math, mostly limited to combinatorics
  • astronomy
  • cosmology, or quantum mechanics, or the nebulous term known as theoretical physics
